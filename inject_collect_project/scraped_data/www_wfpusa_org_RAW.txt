# Donor Portal
Donation payments before Nov. 27th, 2024 will not be visible. We will email an annual tax receipt in February 2025. Please email giving@wfpusa.org to request previous giving history and we will get back to you in 3 – 5 business days.
### Manage My World Food Program USA Giving
You can manage many aspects of your giving through our  donor portal . Simply enter your email and receive a link to login, there is no password to remember.
LOGIN Donate
### What you can manage in your donor portal:
Profile:  You can edit your name and addresses in the portal for your gift receipts. Donation History:  In the portal, your giving history to World Food Program USA is available for you to download by individual donations or a complete list of a given year. Recurring Donation:  If you have a recurring donation, you can use the donor portal to change your donation amount, move the installment date, change your payment method or pause or cancel your donation. Please note the following:
- To access your donor portal you must use the email you entered when donating.
- Donation payments before Nov. 27th, 2024 will not be visible. Please email  giving@wfpusa.org  to request previous giving history and we will get back to you in 3 – 5 business days.
- The donor portal does not apply to donations where the method of payment is cash, check or gift of stock.
