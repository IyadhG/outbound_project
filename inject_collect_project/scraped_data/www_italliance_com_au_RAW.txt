# Trusted ICT Talent for Government & Defence Projects.
We deliver security-cleared professionals for mission-critical roles.
IT Alliance Australia is a proudly Australian-owned, Canberra-based SME, and winner of the Australian Small Business Champion Award for Recruitment two years in a row. Established in December 2016, we are an ISO 27001:2022 certified ICT recruitment company committed to powering Australia’s digital future. We specialise in the delivery of high-quality ICT contract and permanent staffing solutions across the Australian federal government, state and territory Governments, local councils, universities and educational institutions, and private sector organisations nationwide.
Headquartered in Barton, ACT, with operations across all major cities in Australia, we are committed to helping government and enterprise clients build strong, secure, and scalable ICT teams. Our strength lies in our strong social network and internal database of over 20,000 pre-screened Australian citizens, many of whom hold active security clearances (Baseline, NV1, NV2), enabling us to meet highly regulated project demands quickly and reliably.
As a Defence Industry Security Program (DISP)–member, we can sponsor eligible candidates with the right skills and capability to obtain and maintain Baseline and NV1 security clearances — opening doors to exclusive, career-defining opportunities.
